About VaxGuard AI

VaxGuard AI is a revolutionary solution designed to prevent vaccine wastage by ensuring the integrity of the cold-chain. Our system combines a physical, irreversible thermochromic label with a sophisticated, embedded Graphene sensor that provides real-time temperature monitoring and predictive analysis.

We aim to protect the health of millions of newborns and save billions in financial losses by providing healthcare workers with instant, verifiable data on vaccine viability.

Our Technology

Dual-Layer Guardian

Our system uses a dual-layered approach for maximum reliability:

  • Thermochromic Label: An irreversible, self-adhesive label that changes color from green to red the moment a vial exceeds $8^{\circ}C$, providing an instant visual cue to frontline workers.
  • Graphene Sensor: An embedded sensor records temperature every minute with an accuracy of $\pm0.1^{\circ}C$. The data is stored offline for up to 72 hours and auto-syncs via LoRa or 4G.

Predictive AI & Blockchain

The data collected by the sensor is analyzed by our predictive AI model to identify potential cold-chain failures before they occur. This allows for proactive intervention.

Additionally, the entire cold-chain journey is recorded and timestamped on a blockchain, ensuring an immutable record and providing complete transparency and integrity of the data.
